Apocalypse Vagina







Apocalypse Vagina is electronic music

Apocalypse Vagina is not only about music

It's about the emotions of our fragile generation, marked by uncertainty, yet unbroken and capable of rebirth

We are in connection with all the souls out there who have experienced a personal apocalypse

We want to inject new energy to emerge from this, as life is a place where one can always be born again




Our music is a fusion of analog synth and computer music, inspired by Jungle, Techno, Future Bass, Dark Synth, and Ambient.

Our live performance, composed by analog synthesizers, drum set and controllers, transports listeners to a dystopian dimension though deep harmonics, contrasted by the rhythmic foundations that exude energy and confidence
